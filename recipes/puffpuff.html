PUFFPUFF RECIPE

DESCRIPTION

Ingredients

  • 200g Flour
  • 100g Sugar
  • 1 cup of lukewarm water
  • 4g of baking soda
  • 1 tsp of salt

Steps

  1. Mix the flour and sugar
  2. Then add the baking soda and salt with your first mix
  3. Stir the dry mixture
  4. Add the water progressively in to the mixture and mix thouroughly
  5. Leave to rest for 30 minutes
  6. Heat oil to 200C and fry the mixture
  7. Your PuffPuff is ready
