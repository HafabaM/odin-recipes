GOYAVA SMOOTHIE

DESCRIPTION

Ingredients

  • 5 GOYAVA
  • 50g of sugar
  • 50g of yoghurt
  • 1 banana
  • 1 cup of water

Steps

  1. Put all the ingredients in a blender and blend for 30 seconds
  2. Pour the smoothie in a glass and store in the fridge for at least 1 hour
  3. Serve cold, it's ready
