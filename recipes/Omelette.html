OMELETTE

DESCRIPTION

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 30 cl of olive oil
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tsp of salt

Steps

  1. Chop the tomato and onion in to small dices
  2. break the eggs and whip them with all the remaining ingredients
  3. Heat oil and fry the mixture
  4. Your Omelette is ready
